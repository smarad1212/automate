Personalized Lesson Plan for Rani - Chiang Mai Adventure

Location: Chiang Mai | Day: Tuesday | Age: 7 | Interests: Soccer | Language: Hebrew

00:00

Introduction (10 minutes)

Discuss the day's plan and get Rani excited about learning about Chiang Mai and its culture while incorporating his interests and Hebrew language skills.

What are you most excited to learn about today, Rani?

Reading Passage (15 minutes)

Read a short story about a young boy visiting Chiang Mai and experiencing the local culture, such as trying Thai food, visiting temples, and interacting with elephants. Encourage Rani to follow along and ask questions.

What was your favorite part of the story?

Hebrew and English Activity (20 minutes)

Review the Hebrew soccer vocabulary from the previous lesson and introduce new words related to Chiang Mai attractions, such as:

  • פִּיל (pil) - elephant
  • מִקְדָּשׁ (mikdash) - temple
  • שׁוּק (shuk) - market

Play a memory game where Rani matches the Hebrew words with their English translations.

How many word pairs did you match correctly?
